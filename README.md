## 🛡 Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention
**Part A – Case Study:** Phishing Attack  
Described a phishing scenario where attackers trick victims into sharing sensitive information via fake websites. Students are highly vulnerable due to high digital activity. Consequences include financial loss 💸, identity theft 🆔, and account compromise.  

**Part B – Prevention Checklist:**  
- 🚫 Never click on suspicious links.  
- 🔍 Verify sender addresses.  
- 🔑 Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).  
- 💰 Keep UPI PINs and passwords secure. *(Financial Tip)*  
- 📶 Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking. *(Financial Tip)*  
- 🔒 Use strong, unique passwords.  
- 🛡 Update devices and antivirus software.  
- 🧑‍💻 Limit personal info on social media.  
- 📞 Report suspicious activity via [National Cyber Crime Portal](https://cybercrime.gov.in) / Helpline 1930.  
- 📚 Educate peers about scams.  

**💡 Reflection:** I was surprised at how easily phishing can exploit trust. I will now always verify links and enable 2FA on every online account.  
**📂 Output:** Case study and checklist saved in `task-5-cybercrime/`.
