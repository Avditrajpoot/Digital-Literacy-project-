# 📱 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## ✅ Do’s
- ✅ Maintain a professional profile on **LinkedIn**  
- ✅ Think before posting or commenting online 🤔  
- ✅ Use **privacy settings** to control who sees your content 🔒  
- ✅ Share educational or skill-building content 📚💡  
- ✅ Fact-check information before sharing 🕵️‍♂️  

## ❌ Don’ts
- ❌ Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content 🚫  
- ❌ Don’t share personal information publicly 🛑  
- ❌ Don’t engage in online arguments ⚡  
- ❌ Don’t upload sensitive work-related documents 🗂️  
- ❌ Don’t use social media during lectures or meetings unnecessarily ⏰
