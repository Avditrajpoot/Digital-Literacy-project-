# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

Follow these steps to protect yourself from phishing, scams, and other online threats:  

1. ⚠️ **Never click on suspicious links** from emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages.  
2. 🔍 **Verify the sender’s email address or phone number** before responding.  
3. 🔑 **Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)** for all online accounts.  
4. 💳 **Do not share UPI PINs, OTPs, or banking passwords** with anyone. *(Financial Safety Tip)*  
5. 🛡️ **Use strong, unique passwords** for different platforms and update them regularly.  
6. 📶 **Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.** *(Financial Safety Tip)*  
7. 📝 **Report any phishing or suspicious activity** to the [National Cyber Crime Portal](https://cybercrime.gov.in) or call Helpline 1930.  
8. 💻 **Keep your devices and antivirus software updated** to protect against malware.  
9. 👤 **Limit personal information shared on social media** to avoid identity theft.  
10. 📚 **Educate yourself and peers** about common online scams and digital threats.

---

**Tip:** This checklist is ideal for students and young adults who frequently use online platforms. Sharing these tips with peers can help build a safer digital community. ✅
